Process

What this mode does

The app extracts a summary, pulls out reusable ideas, suggests actions, assigns tags, stores the note, and refreshes its embeddings.

Best input: a raw note, not a polished paragraph.
Typical sources: highlighted passages, spoken reflections, OCR from pages, quick paraphrases.
Result: one durable note that is easier to retrieve later than the original capture.
